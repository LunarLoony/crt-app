CRT is the portal to your game collection. With its straightforward interface and sleek design, it's a great way to organise (and play) your digital game collection.

Creating using Node.JS, Electron, and some good old-fashioned HTML and CSS, CRT is simple and snappy, helping you get to the important part: gaming.
